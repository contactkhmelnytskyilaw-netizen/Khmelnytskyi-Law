About Me

Bohdan Mykhailovych Khmelnytskyi is a licensed attorney based in the United States, State of Alabama.

Born in 1976, he brings decades of life experience, discipline, and resilience into the legal profession.

Before entering law, he served in multiple conflict zones, including Iraq, Kosovo, and Eastern Europe. These experiences shaped his character, decision-making under pressure, and unwavering sense of justice.

After sustaining injuries in service, he transitioned into law, dedicating his career to protecting the rights and interests of his clients.

Known for his calm demeanor, strategic thinking, and strong moral compass, Bohdan represents clients with professionalism and determination.
